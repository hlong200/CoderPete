    Act like an adult:

    • Respectful
      • Be at your computer when bell rings
      • Pay Attention:
        • No side talking during class discussion
        • No headphones
      • Stay at your computer unless you have permission to move around.
      • Keep your feet on the floor.
    • Responsible
      • Stay on task
      • Once you have completed assignments and homework, you may:
        • Do homework for other classes
        • Read a book
        • Visit quietly with your neighbors
        • Things pre-arranged with instructor
    • Safe
      • No horseplay:
      • Handle lab equipment with care
      • Only work with technology to which you are assigned
      • Stay out of utility room

    Other Important Policies

    • No Food or Drink (Sealed Water bottles only!)

    • No Video Games

    • No image searching without permission

    • No cell phones

Computer Lab Do's and Dont's

  • Do not bring Food or drinks into the lab.
  • Do use surge protectors.
  • Do not mess with the Dell computers.
    This includes mice, keyboards, monitors, and cases
  • Do not Plug or unplug anything into the network.
  • No games except those listed on this page.
